Complete Guide on FlexBox

As a job seeker, you may wonder To understand flexbox, first understand the concept of a main axis and a cross axis. The main axis is the one set by your flex-direction property, along which all flex items are laid out. If that is a row, your main axis is along the row; if that is a column, your main axis is along the column. The axis perpendicular to the main axis is called the cross axis. Its direction depends on the main axis's direction.
